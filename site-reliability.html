Site Reliability Engineering

A list of various site reliability projects that I have created over time:

  • awesome-sre: A curated list of Site Reliability and Production Engineering resources.
  • awesome-chaos-engineering: A curated list of Chaos Engineering resources.
  • postmortem-templates: A collection of postmortem templates.
  • Availability Calculator: Calculate how much downtime should be permitted in your Service Level Agreement or Objective.
  • Error Budget Calculator: Calculate the tolerable downtime of your service.
  • Wheel of Misfortune: Role Playing Game for Incident Management Training.
  • SRE Workbook - Markdown Templates: A collection of ported Markdown templates from the SRE Workbook.
  • Cards Against Reliability: A fun card game for software engineers and SREs, based on Cards Against Humanity.
